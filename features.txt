- View Full profile feature for Staff and Students where admin or staff
    would be able to check all the details of the student and staff including 
    their records like results, test scores, last logins etc.
- Admin can check the school's overall analysis e.g yearly analysis of overall 
student performance, overall increase or decrease in students and staff.

- Staff can check the analysis and historical data of their class
like the overall performance of students, the number of students offering each particular subject. 
- Parents can give feedback.
